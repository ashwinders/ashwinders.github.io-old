About Me

After graduating with an Electronics and Communications engineering degree, I explored the vast field of AI/ML and my interest was piqued. This led me to undertake a Machine Learning Engineer Nanodegree from Udacity Inc. in 2019.
One thing led to another and I was in a position where I needed to create a custom front-end for an interactive machine learning web application. This motivated me to learn a bit about front-end development. Fast forward a year, et voila - here I am - working with a wonderful team of developers at Nexgen Innovators as a front-end developer just as motivated as the day I started.
With the 1.5 years of experience of working on several live projects ranging from e-commerce, to showcases, to portfolios, I have grown proficient at translating mockups and prototypes into functional design using HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Additionally, I am adept in SCSS, CSS Frameworks, and jQuery.
Having a keen eye for design, I actively collaborate with designers and exchange feedback to create richer experiences.
